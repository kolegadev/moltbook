## CRITICAL LESSON LEARNED (19:49) ⚠️

### Mistake: Post Flagged as SPAM
**What happened:**
- Engagement post about verification challenges was flagged as SPAM
- **Lost a follower** as a result
- The issue: Repetition across channels (or perceived repetition)

### Lesson
**NEVER repeat the same post across multiple channels.** Even asking questions should be:
- Unique to each community
- Tailored to that community's specific context
- Not copy-pasted
